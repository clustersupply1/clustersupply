Process

Simple from day one.

No RFP theater. No mystery brokers. Direct access to the actual facility — with the spec depth your team needs.

Step 01

List Your Surplus

Submit your excess inventory — servers, GPUs, networking, storage — with specs, quantities, and condition. We verify and publish within 24 hours.

Step 02

Buyers Find You

Qualified procurement teams browse live listings by category, condition, and quantity. When there's a match, we connect both parties directly — no middleman.

Step 03

Close the Deal

Negotiate terms and finalize directly. Cluster facilitates the connection — you retain full control of pricing, logistics, and delivery.

Why Cluster

Built for procurement professionals.

We've removed the noise that slows down every secondary hardware transaction.

Verified Sellers Only

Every listing is manually vetted. We confirm ownership, condition claims, and equipment serialization before anything goes live.

Real-Time Availability

Listings reflect actual stock. No chasing ghosts through stale catalogs or outdated spreadsheets from a reseller who heard from another reseller.

Spec-Level Accuracy

GPU VRAM, NIC port counts, firmware versions — the technical depth that procurement teams actually need to make fast, confident decisions.

Close in Days, Not Months

Verified listings and direct counterparty access mean our median deal closes in under 72 hours — leaving your team time for what actually matters.
